# Google Resume Guidelines for LLM

## Format Requirements

- PDF output
- No objective statement
- Black text, clean simple consistent font
- No typos
- Bullet points only (no long descriptive text)
- Include contact info + GitHub link (for technical positions)
- **Maximum 1 page** (exception: technical/engineering with many relevant projects may use 2 pages)
- Tailored for each specific position
- If a bullet point spills to second line by 1-2 words, rewrite shorter

## Content Structure

### For Recent Graduates

- Education before experience
- Include: school, degree, major, GPA, graduation month/year

### For Experienced Professionals

- Experience before education (reverse chronological)
- Less college detail as time passes
- Cut older/irrelevant positions to maintain 1-page limit
- Consider brief mention of other less-relevant experience without detail

### For Technical Positions

- GitHub link at top with contact info
- Programming languages listed prominently
- Languages used listed per project/experience

## X-Y-Z Formula

Every accomplishment should follow: **Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]**

Focus on quantitative results and impact.

### Examples

**Membership:**

- OK: "Member of Leadership for Tomorrow Society"
- Better: "Selected as one of 275 for this 12-month professional development program for high-achieving diverse talent"
- Best: "Selected as one of 275 participants nationwide for this 12-month professional development program for high-achieving diverse talent based on leadership potential and academic success"

**Technical Achievement:**

- OK: "Won second place in hackathon"
- Better: "Won second place out of 50 teams in hackathon"
- Best: "Won second place out of 50 teams in hackathon at NJ Tech by working with two colleagues to develop an app that synchronizes mobile calendars"

**Business Impact:**

- OK: "Grew revenue for small and medium business clients"
- Better: "Grew revenue for small and medium business clients by 10% QoQ"
- Best: "Grew revenue for 15 small and medium business clients by 10% QoQ by mapping new software features as solutions to their business goals"

### Jargon Rule

Only use shorthand (like "QoQ") if 100% certain reviewer will understand

## Key Principles

- Ruthless editing for space efficiency
- Quantify everything possible
- Show impact and results, not just responsibilities
- Most relevant items first
- Recent/relevant over comprehensive
